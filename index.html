A birthday letter

My Dearest Desre

My Dearest Desre,

There are people who come into our lives for a season, and then there are the rare souls who become part of our hearts forever. You are one of those rare people.

As I sat down to write this letter, I realised that words will never truly be enough to express how grateful I am for you, but I hope they give you even the smallest glimpse into how much you mean to me.

Thank you for being such an important part of my life. Thank you for choosing to walk beside me through life’s beautiful moments and its difficult ones. Your friendship has been one of the greatest blessings I could ever ask for.

Thank you for your unwavering love and unconditional friendship. Thank you for always having my back, even when I didn’t have the strength to stand on my own. Knowing that I have someone like you in my corner has given me courage more times than you’ll ever know.

Thank you for always listening—not just hearing my words, but truly listening with your heart. You have a remarkable way of making people feel understood, valued, and never alone. Whether I needed someone to celebrate life’s victories with me or simply someone to sit quietly beside me during the hard days, you have always been there.

Thank you for caring so deeply. Your kindness isn’t something you switch on and off—it is simply who you are. You love without expecting anything in return, and you give so much of yourself to the people you care about.

Thank you for your wisdom and the countless pieces of advice you have shared over the years. Somehow you always know exactly what to say, whether it is a gentle word of encouragement, a little reality check, or simply reminding me that tomorrow is another day. Your words have carried me through more than you will ever realise.

Thank you for the laughter we have shared, for the memories we have created, for the tears we have wiped away, and for all the moments in between. Every conversation, every coffee, every phone call, and every hug has become another precious thread woven into the beautiful story of our friendship.

Most of all, thank you for simply being you.

Thank you for your beautiful heart, your generosity, your compassion, your strength, and your incredible ability to make everyone around you feel loved. The world is a brighter, kinder, and more beautiful place because you are in it.

I hope you never forget how extraordinary you are. I hope you always remember that you are deeply loved, endlessly appreciated, and treasured by so many people—including me.

I honestly cannot imagine my life without your friendship, and I thank God for bringing you into my world. You have made my life richer, happier, and infinitely more meaningful simply by being part of it.

As you celebrate another year of your beautiful life, my wish for you is that you receive even a fraction of the love, kindness, and joy that you so freely give to everyone else. You deserve every beautiful blessing that comes your way.

Happy Birthday, my beautiful friend.

Never change who you are, because the person you are is exactly the person this world needs.

With all my love, always,
Claudia ❤️
